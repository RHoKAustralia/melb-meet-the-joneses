Share what's spare

Collaborative Consumption promotes smarter, efficient use of stuff. New peer-to-peer platforms, (like airbnb, the world's leading community driven hospitality company) connect anyone with an idle surfboard, house, car or service, with someone who wants them, enabling the sharing of resources for maximum efficiency. (On average, airbnb guests use 63% less energy than hotel guests - or enough to power 19,000 homes for a year!)

Visit airbnb for an example of a collaborative economy company.
