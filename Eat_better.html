Eat Healthier

While going meat-free (and halving our carbon footprint) isn't for everyone, reducing how much meat we eat is better for us and our planet. (98% of antibiotics sold for animal use in Australia go into the animals we eat, while 51% of greenhouse gas emissions come from livestock farming & related activities.) With Meat Free Monday on The New Joneses' menu, they love the delicious, protein-rich, meat-free options from Fry's Family Foods. The family business of committed vegetarians, source ingredients ethically and sustainably. (No palm oil in these patties!)

Love food, hate waste

Victorian households toss away $4 billion worth of edible food a year. Throwing away food wastes money, impacts our environment (food in landfill produces methane, a potent greenhouse gas) and wastes all the resources (water, soils and energy) used to grow, process, package and transport it. The New Joneses shop to a list, buy what they need, get creative with leftovers and compost the rest.

Learn more by visiting Love food, hate waste (link opens in a new window).
